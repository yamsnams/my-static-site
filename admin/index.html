Visitor diary submissions from the Submit page

Since external services like Netlify have been removed, public submissions cannot be automatically saved to your GitHub.

Instead, the submit.html page now opens the visitor's email client (via a mailto: link) to email the diary entry directly to you.

How it works: When you receive a diary entry via email, simply copy it and go to the Diary Entries tab to publish it manually. This ensures a 100% private, zero-cost, serverless setup.
